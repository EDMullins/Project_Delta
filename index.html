Why Do Ads Suck?

The Problem with Mobile Game Ads

+ Have you ever tried to close an ad in a mobile game, only to tap through several screens before finally returning to your game? Many ads are intentionally designed to make exiting confusing.

  • tiny “X” buttons
  • fake close icons
  • and extra pop-ups that remove you from the game

+ These unnecessary steps waste time and frustrate users. Each extra click and delay breaks the flow of gameplay and goes against core design principles like clarity, efficiency, and user control.

+ This project demonstrates that problem by showing:

  • a BAD AD FLOW with multiple screens to exit
  • an improved GOOD AD FLOW that allows users to skip or close the ad quickly and confidently

+ Goal: Reduce clicks, shorten time to exit, and make ad interactions more transparent and user-friendly.
